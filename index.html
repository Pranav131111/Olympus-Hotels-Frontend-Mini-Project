About our Hotel

History of " Olympus Hotels "

Founded in 1998, Olympus Hotel draws its inspiration from the rich tapestry of Greek mythology. Established with a vision to emulate the grandeur of Mount Olympus, the legendary dwelling of the gods, our hotel embodies the spirit of timeless elegance and divine hospitality.
